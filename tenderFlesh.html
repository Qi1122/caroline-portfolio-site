Tender Flesh

/2025

Introduction

Tender Flesh is a photographic exploration of anxiety as a self-consuming ritual. Through four intimate frames, the artist examines how emotional distress finds expression in the body—specifically, in the compulsive act of skin picking.

This series suggests that anxiety does not simply gnaw at the mind—it consumes the body. Each photograph reveals a layer of vulnerability beneath the skin, asking: what are we truly feeding when we chew on our own pain?
