Beside The Sky - BTS

/2024

Introduction

Beside the Sky is a student film directed by Junfan Li, a student at the School of Visual Arts (SVA), and produced by Cheung Xu . The film was shot in January 2024, capturing scenes in locations spanning New Jersey, Queens, and Long Island. In the course of this project, I had the privilege of serving as the behind-the-scenes (BTS) photographer. In this role, I meticulously documented the intricacies of the production process, capturing candid moments, interactions, and the overall atmosphere on set. My responsibilities extended beyond the technical aspects of photography; I aimed to convey the essence of the project through my lens, providing a visual narrative that complemented the creative vision of the team. This experience allowed me to not only contribute to the documentation of the project's journey but also immerse myself in the dynamic and collaborative world of film production.
